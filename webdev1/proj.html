Top Call of Duty E-Sports Teams

An introduction to Call of duty E-sports teams. This is a list of who I think have been the best organizations to compete in Call of Duty for the past five years. There are a lot of good teams left out so this is my personal opinion on who should be mentioned as the top of the top, creme de la creme.

Optic Gaming

"Known as the Green Wall". Also rising from their popularity in creating sniping montages when sniping was what everyone did. They quickly put their foot in the door into the competitive scene and was automatically a fan favorite popular with the gaming scene. Having big names who created content such as Nadeshot, who now is owner of 100 Thieves, and other content creators like BigTymer and Scump created a loyal fanbase. In my opinion, they will always be Call of Duty's favorite team.
